About Me

I’m Cara, a QA Software Engineer focused on ensuring product quality through detailed testing, bug tracking, and collaboration. I’ve contributed to multiple live bug jams, and also take initiative to retest and validate fixes.

Tools I use include JIRA, Postman, Selenium, TestRail, and more. I love digging into issues and making products better.

What is a QA Engineer?
A QA (Quality Assurance) Engineer is responsible for ensuring that software products meet quality standards before they’re released. This involves planning and executing tests, identifying bugs, and working closely with developers to resolve issues. The purpose is to prevent defects, enhance product performance, and deliver the best user experience possible.
